About us

Welcome to TravelLab, your number one source for all things travel. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of service, with a focus on luxury holiday packages, affordable prices, and a friendly service.

Founded in 2019 by James, TravelLab has come a long way from its beginnings in Northern Ireland. When James first started out, his passion for luxury trips at an affordable price drove him to quit his day job so that TravelLab can offer you the best quality holidays at real-world prices. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our holidays as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
